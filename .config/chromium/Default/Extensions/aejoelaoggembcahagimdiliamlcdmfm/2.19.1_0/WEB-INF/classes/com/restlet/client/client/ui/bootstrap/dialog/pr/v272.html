July 31, 2017 - Restlet Client 2.7.2

You’ve got mail from the Postman

Postman and Restlet Client (f.k.a. DHC) are great tools and have been around for years. In the past you might have used Postman to do stuff we did not support at the time and maybe you still have some of your data there.

For this reason and starting now, you’ll be able to import your Postman collections (v2.0) into Restlet Client and play with them.

Of course, as Green Lantern would say: On earth we have a saying “we’re just human”. If you think some of your data were Lost in translation, feel free to provide us any feedback .
